What is METAR?

METAR data is a form of weather data that comes in standardized human readable (really!) text. Pilots and meteorologists can glance at a page of METAR data and get an accurate 3D picture of the weather over huge regions and altitudes.

It does take some effort to learn, but it's worth it.
